# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

UCGO-DEV is a preservation and reverse engineering project. We want it to be a
place where people with genuine interest in UCGO — whether researchers, developers,
or players — can collaborate without friction.

We are committed to keeping this community open, respectful, and focused on the work.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussion
- Engage honestly — if you're unsure about something, say so
- Give credit when building on someone else's findings
- Keep discussion relevant to the project
- Welcome contributors regardless of their experience level

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Gatekeeping or dismissing contributions without explanation
- Sharing another contributor's private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or pull request reviews

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this project.

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact
the project maintainer directly. Reports will be handled with discretion.

Contributors who violate this Code of Conduct may be warned or removed from the
project at the maintainer's discretion.

---

## Note on the Project's Nature

This project involves reverse engineering for preservation purposes. Discussion of
technical methods, encryption, and protocol analysis is expected and encouraged.

What is not acceptable is using this project's work, tools, or community to cause
harm — to individuals, other projects, or the broader UCGO community.
